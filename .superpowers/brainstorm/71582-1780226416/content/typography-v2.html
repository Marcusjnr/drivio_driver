Common thread

None of these are in the default kit. Each adds a small craft element to the wordmark (an accent dot, hairline-flanked all-caps, a heritage mark). The body fonts are all less ubiquitous than the first round — Onest, Hanken Grotesk, Albert Sans are real workhorses without being everywhere.

If none of these feel right either, the next move is either (a) commissioning a custom wordmark, or (b) paid foundry fonts (PP Editorial Old, Söhne, Migra, etc) — both require deciding outside this brainstorming flow. Tell me what's still missing.
